Fridge failures have patterns

Absorption fridges (Dometic, Norcold) fail in known ways — boiler, cooling unit, control board. A proper diagnosis tells you whether to repair, swap to 12V, or replace.

Heat & hot water before the trip

Furnace and water heater failures show up the first cold night out. A pre-trip check at home is cheaper than an emergency call from a campground.

Propane handled properly

LP appliances deserve someone who works with gas systems constantly. Leak checks and pressure tests come with the territory.
